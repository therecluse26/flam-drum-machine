Free drum kits

FlamKit ships with curated CC0 (public-domain) kits — no attribution required, no restrictions, free for any use including commercial. Every kit's provenance is verified before it's listed.

Launch kits are in final curation. The v1.0 starter kits are being verified against our CC0 provenance bar and packaged as flamkit.yaml kits. The finished list — with per-kit provenance notes and download links — publishes here at launch. Join the community to hear when they drop.

Our quality & licensing bar

Every official kit must clear the same bar before it ships. This is non-negotiable.

StandardWhat we guarantee
LicenseCC0 (public domain) or another GPL-compatible, attribution-optional license. No Non-Commercial restrictions — ever.
ProvenanceSource verified before listing. No item appears without a documented, verifiable origin.
Audio24-bit, 44.1/48 kHz, WAV. Zero decode overhead for real-time disk streaming.
CoverageMulti-velocity layers with round-robins so repeated hits never sound like a machine gun.

Distributed by design

Anyone can host a kit repository.

FlamKit's kit distribution is decentralized, inspired by Linux package managers. The official repo is curated and high-quality — but you're never limited to it. Host your own kits, share the URL, and (from v1.1) browse and auto-update kits right inside the plugin.
