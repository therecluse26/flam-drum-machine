MuldjordKit (FreePats edition) — License & Attribution
======================================================

Kit:        MuldjordKit — FreePats stereo edition, version 2020-10-18
            (derived from the original 16-channel DrumGizmo MuldjordKit v3.0,
            released August 7th 2018).
Instrument: Tama Superstar acoustic kit — 2 kickdrums, 3 hanging/rack toms,
            1 floor tom, 1 snare, 1 hi-hat, 2 crash cymbals, 2 ride cymbals,
            1 china cymbal. Recorded by Lars Muldjord during the 2009 Sepulchrum
            drum sessions. A rock / metal kit.
Source:     FreePats project
            Page:     https://freepats.zenvoid.org/Percussion/acoustic-drum-kit.html
            Repo:     https://github.com/freepats/muldjordkit
            Original: DrumGizmo project — https://drumgizmo.org/ (www.muldjord.com)
Creator:    Lars Muldjord (kit, microphone setup, sampling, editing).
            FreePats stereo edition assembled by Roberto (roberto@zenvoid.org).

MODIFICATIONS MADE BY FLAM:
  - Repackaged from the FreePats SFZ instrument into FlamKit's `flamkit.yaml`
    format for use in the FlamKit drum engine.
  - Drum pieces were mapped onto the General MIDI percussion layout (see
    README.md). Two extra articulations (a second snare-rest and a second ride
    bell) were placed on otherwise-empty acoustic-kit MIDI slots, documented in
    README.md.
  - Audio sample files (WAV) are unmodified from the FreePats release.
