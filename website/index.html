Why FlamKit

Professional drums shouldn't cost $500.

Commercial drum samplers run $300–$1000+. FlamKit gives the bedroom producer and the professional studio the same world-class engine — free, forever.

🥁

Sounds pro out of the box

Curated CC0 kits — punchy electronic machines and natural acoustic kits — with multi-velocity layers and round-robins so nothing sounds like a machine gun.

⚡

Fast & native

Built in C++20 on JUCE. Hybrid streaming keeps a full kit at 50–100 MB RAM (vs. 1–2 GB), with sample-accurate timing under 5 ms at 64-sample buffers.

🔓

Truly open

GPLv3. Human-readable flamkit.yaml kits you can edit in any text editor. Read the source, file an issue, shape the roadmap — no black boxes.

Multi-output routing

A virtual multitrack drum session.

Each microphone channel — kick close, snare top, overheads, room — routes to its own DAW track. Mix with your favourite native plugins, exactly like a real recording session.

1–16 channels per hit

Sample as many mics as you tracked. FlamKit detects the channel count from the kit and exposes the buses automatically.

Full DAW automation

Every parameter is exposed for automation. Treat FlamKit like a hardware unit in your session.

Built-in mixer fallback

Per-channel volume, pan, solo and mute for standalone use or hosts without multi-output.

For developers

Want to build your own? Ship a plugin in an afternoon.

FlamKit is a clean, fully documented JUCE codebase built around AudioProcessorGraph. Clone it, build it, and use it as the reference for shipping your own VST. GPLv3 keeps the whole ecosystem open.
