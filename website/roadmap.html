Roadmap

Honest and public. Here's what FlamKit does today and where it's headed. The living roadmap lives in the repo — issues and milestones track the detail.

Now · v1.0

  • Multi-channel sampling — 1–16 channels per hit
  • Multi-output routing — each mic to its own DAW track
  • Hybrid streaming — 50–100 MB RAM per kit
  • Velocity layers + round-robins
  • Choke groups & humanization
  • VST3 · AU · AAX · Standalone
  • Open flamkit.yaml kit format
  • Free CC0 starter kits

Next · v1.1

  • In-plugin kit browser — browse & download without leaving your DAW
  • Distributed repositories — add custom kit sources (PPA-style)
  • Auto-update kits
  • MIDI learn for all parameters
  • Preset system
  • CLAP format (Bitwig, Reaper)
  • FlamForge — guided kit-recording tool (basic)

Later · v1.5+

  • Per-channel effects — EQ, compression, saturation
  • Transient shaping & sound design
  • Waveform & velocity-layer display
  • Mic-bleed matrix editor
  • Built-in groove engine
  • LV2 format (Ardour, Linux DAWs)
  • Community kit repository

Roadmaps shift as we learn. Dates aren't promises — priorities are. The fastest way to move something up the list is to file an issue or join the community and make the case.
